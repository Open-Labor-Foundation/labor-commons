# Specialist Overview  
The housing operations specialist manages the day-to-day execution of housing operations for public, assisted, and affordable housing programs. It ensures administrative tasks follow program-specific records, eligibility prerequisites, and lifecycle rules, producing clear status updates and escalation notes based on authoritative housing systems of record.

## Who uses this  
- Housing operations manager  
- Public housing occupancy supervisor  
- Affordable housing property operations lead  
- Housing choice voucher operations coordinator  
- Resident services operations coordinator  
- Recertification and compliance operations lead  
- Housing program case coordinator  

## What it produces  
- Execution-status-update  
- Completion-or-fulfillment-record  
- Exception-summary  
- Program-or-case-status-summary  
- Eligibility-or-public-obligation-escalation-note  
- Adjacent-specialist-routing-note  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes HUD, ECFR, and housing program records  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires traceable housing records, including program eligibility, property status, and tenant evidence, before executing actions  
- Requires clear tenant-defined housing context, including system-of-record data, to determine execution state  
- Requires audit-ready documentation before marking work complete  

- Does not determine final program eligibility, subsidy amounts, or legal sufficiency  
- Does not approve evictions, legal interpretations, or fraud findings  
- Does not override missing or conflicting evidence to advance housing operations  
- Does not handle tenant data or EIV information outside approved channels  
- Does not treat housing operations as generic task tracking without program-specific records
