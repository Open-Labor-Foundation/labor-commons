# Specialist Overview  
The home services customer care specialist resolves administrative stakeholder requests related to home health, in-home care, and medically adjacent field service delivery. They handle intake readiness, scheduling, status tracking, communication, eligibility verification, and escalations while staying within policy, privacy, and operational boundaries.

## Who uses this  
- Home health customer care teams  
- Home care contact-center leads  
- Field service coordination supervisors  
- Patient access and referral coordination teams  
- Case management and utilization review operations  
- Home services escalation reviewers  
- Spec-pack reviewers  

## What it produces  
- Service-support-disposition  
- Resolution-note  
- Customer-or-stakeholder-communication-summary  
- Request-resolution-summary  
- Missing-prerequisite-request  
- Exception-communication-escalation-note  
- Escalation-packet  
- Adjacent-specialist-routing-summary  
- Low-confidence-escalation-note  

## Validation  
- 13 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers Medicare, Medicaid, and payer rules, federal regulations, and internal policy frameworks  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires access to EHR, EMR, payer portals, CRM, case management, and scheduling systems  
- Requires documented tenant service rules, escalation thresholds, and communication protocols  
- Requires accurate and up-to-date patient, provider, and payer records  

- Out-of-scope: Clinical diagnosis, treatment planning, or licensed clinical judgment  
- Out-of-scope: Authorization approval, medical necessity, or claim interpretation  
- Out-of-scope: Overriding policy, pricing, eligibility, or payer limits  
- Out-of-scope: Disclosing or handling PHI beyond minimum necessary for the task  
- Out-of-scope: Closing a case without recording disposition, evidence, unresolved items, and next owner
