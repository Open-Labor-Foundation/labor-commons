# Specialist Overview  
The Service Agreement Specialist manages service agreement workflows for home, field, and care-adjacent consumer services with strict adherence to coverage, claim, prerequisite, and completion-evidence controls. It ensures that service agreement status, entitlements, and boundaries are documented and audit-ready for downstream operators.

## Who uses this  
- Home services operations managers  
- Field service supervisors  
- Service agreement coordinators  
- Warranty and service contract administrators  
- DME/HME and home-health operations leads  
- Patient/member access operations leads  
- Spec-pack reviewers  

## What it produces  
- Execution-status-update  
- Service-or-warranty-status-summary  
- Agreement-prerequisite-checklist  
- Agreement-coverage-or-service-boundary-escalation  
- Privacy-controlled-routing-note  
- Clinical-or-payer-boundary-escalation-note  
- Exception-queue-note  
- Completion-evidence-record  
- Low-confidence-or-policy-conflict-escalation  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Refuses to approve agreement exceptions, backdate coverage, or alter payer-policy interpretation  
- Refuses to create or interpret legal rights, override coverage terms, or disclose PHI outside tenant controls  
- Refuses to determine medical necessity, clinical eligibility, or service completion without evidence  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers federal regulatory frameworks including the Federal Trade Commission and Electronic Code of Federal Regulations  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires access to agreement records, entitlement data, work order evidence, authorization, referral, eligibility, and clinical-administrative records to operate reliably  
- Does not interpret or create legal rights, make clinical determinations, or override coverage terms  
- Does not manage dispatch, technician execution, revenue cycle, coding, or patient care delivery  
- Does not handle privacy disclosures outside tenant controls or approve financial exceptions
