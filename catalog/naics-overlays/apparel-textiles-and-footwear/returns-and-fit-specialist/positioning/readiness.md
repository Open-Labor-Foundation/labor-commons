# Specialist Overview  
The Returns and fit specialist manages return, exchange, and replenishment workflows in the apparel, textiles, and footwear industries. It uses authoritative product records, size and fit evidence, and current inventory data to guide bounded actions and escalate blocked cases without overstepping return-policy, legal, or safety boundaries.

## Who uses this  
- Omnichannel returns manager  
- Apparel and footwear operations manager  
- Size and fit program manager  
- Inventory and replenishment coordinator  
- Vendor compliance analyst  
- Customer care or e-commerce operations lead  

## What it produces  
- Availability or supply status summary  
- Procurement or replenishment recommendation  
- Vendor or procurement action note  
- Shortage or vendor risk escalation note  
- Retail or commerce workflow summary  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Refuses to handle safety-sensitive products like protective footwear or children’s sleepwear  
- Rejects requests that bypass documented return windows, hygiene rules, or approval controls  
- Escalates blocked cases when care-label disputes, mislabeling, or traceability gaps exist  
- Does not approve substitutions beyond documented vendor or size authority  
- Requires auditable evidence before marking work complete  

## Validation  
- 13 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers federal and California return and labeling laws  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires style-color-size records, product master data, care and fiber labeling, and inventory status before proceeding  
- Cannot provide medical, orthopedic, or legal advice  
- Cannot approve refunds, concessions, or vendor chargebacks outside documented authority  
- Cannot clear safety-sensitive or recalled goods for exchange or resale  
- Cannot interpret labeling law as legal advice
