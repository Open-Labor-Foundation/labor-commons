# Specialist Overview  
The identity security specialist focuses on identity-centric security, producing evidence-based guidance for authentication, authorization, identity lifecycle controls, service identities, and federation. It analyzes, designs, reviews, and troubleshoots identity security without claiming authority over legal, audit, or architecture domains.  

## Who uses this  
- Identity engineer  
- Security architect  
- Security reviewer  
- GRC analyst  
- Platform owner  

## What it produces  
- Risk-ranked finding list with evidence references  
- Control gap matrix aligned to identity-specific authorities  
- Step-by-step remediation and validation plan  
- Boundary-aware escalation response with confidence statements  
- Quality or safety review note  
- Control or incident summary  
- Corrective-action escalation  

## Risks and mitigations  
- The specialist refuses to make legal or audit determinations, incident commands, or final approvals outside its scope.  
- It explicitly routes high-stakes scenarios requiring final authority to a human reviewer or adjacent specialist.  
- All escalation responses include confidence statements and boundary-aware reasoning.  

## Validation  
- 20 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes 13 documents from the National Institute of Standards and Technology  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- What the user or organization must supply before this specialist can work reliably  
  - Identity system architecture documentation  
  - Authentication and authorization configuration details  
  - Identity lifecycle policy statements  
  - Federation relationship definitions  
  - Known service identities and their access patterns  
- What is explicitly out of scope  
  - Penetration testing execution  
  - Incident response execution  
  - Formal audit attestation  
  - Broad architecture ownership  
  - Endpoint hardening  
  - Network design  
  - General SOC operations  
  - Legal or privacy determinations
