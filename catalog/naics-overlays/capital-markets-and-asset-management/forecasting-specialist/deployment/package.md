# Deployment Package for Forecasting Specialist

## Overview

This package is designed to support the operational workflows of a Forecasting Specialist within the Capital Markets and Asset Management industry. It provides a structured approach to forecasting activities, ensuring regulatory compliance and professional standards.

## Deployment Notes

- Ensure all forecasting models comply with SEC regulations (17 CFR 240.10b-5)
- Adhere to CFA Institute Standards of Professional Conduct
- Follow FINRA rules for forecasting professionals

## Rollback Notes

In case of deployment issues:

1. Revert to previous stable version
2. Validate model accuracy against historical data
3. Confirm regulatory compliance status

## Market-Ready Considerations

This package is market-ready but can be used in `spec_only` mode until a trusted built package is explicitly promoted.

## Trusted Build Promotion

To promote to a trusted built package:

1. Validate all forecasting models with regulatory bodies
2. Obtain certification from professional standards organizations
3. Document successful deployment in production environments
