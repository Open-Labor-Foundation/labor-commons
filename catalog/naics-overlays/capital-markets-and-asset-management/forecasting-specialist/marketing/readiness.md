# Marketing Readiness for Forecasting Specialist

## Positioning

The Forecasting Specialist overlay is positioned as a specialized solution for professionals in capital markets and asset management who require robust forecasting capabilities while maintaining strict regulatory compliance.

## Target Audience

- Financial analysts
- Asset managers
- Risk managers
- Investment strategists

## Key Benefits

- Regulatory compliance assurance
- Professional standards adherence
- Enhanced forecasting accuracy
- Operational workflow optimization

## Commercialization Notes

- Package supports both spec-only and built-package execution modes
- Ready for immediate deployment in production environments
- Clear upgrade path to trusted built packages

## Packaging Notes

- Includes comprehensive research and functionality mapping
- Provides detailed evaluation scenarios and results
- Contains structured readiness evidence
- Offers clear deployment and rollback procedures
