# Forecasting Specialist Evaluation Scenarios

## Market Volatility Scenario

**Description:** Evaluate forecasting accuracy during periods of high market volatility

**Inputs:**

- Volatile market data

**Expected Outcome:** Accurate forecasts despite market fluctuations

## Regulatory Change Scenario

**Description:** Assess impact of regulatory changes on forecasting models

**Inputs:**

- New regulatory requirements

**Expected Outcome:** Updated forecasts reflecting regulatory compliance

## Economic Downturn Scenario

**Description:** Test forecasting models during economic downturns

**Inputs:**

- Recession data

**Expected Outcome:** Robust forecasts under adverse economic conditions
