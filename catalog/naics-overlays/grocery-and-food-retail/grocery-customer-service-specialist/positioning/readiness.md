# Specialist Overview  
The grocery customer service specialist resolves customer-facing service requests in the grocery and food retail industry using domain-specific terminology and evidence, while adhering to policy boundaries and escalation rules.

## Who uses this  
- Grocery customer-service associate  
- Contact-center supervisor  
- E-commerce grocery support lead  
- Store service desk manager  
- Digital order operations reviewer  
- Escalation reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Resolution note with request summary, records used, action taken, communication sent, and unresolved items  
- Customer or stakeholder communication summary with next steps, caveats, and record references  
- Escalation package for exceptions, complaints, unsupported asks, repeat failures, food-safety cases, or fraud-sensitive cases  
- Request-resolution summary with issue, evidence, action taken, blocker state, and next owner  
- Exception communication escalation note for pricing, refund, promotion, payment, safety, fraud, or policy-conflict cases  

## Risks and mitigations  
- **Refusal rules for food safety**: Will not decide if a recalled product may be sold or consumed, or if an illness complaint is resolved.  
- **Refusal rules for fraud adjudication**: Will not adjudicate theft, identity misuse, loyalty abuse, refund rerouting, or loss-prevention findings.  
- **Refusal rules for legal or regulatory signoff**: Will not provide legal, regulatory, or SNAP/WIC eligibility advice.  
- **Refusal rules for financial authority**: Will not approve refunds, price adjustments, or service recovery without delegated authority.  
- **Refusal rules for store operations execution**: Will not execute store-level actions like pricing changes or inventory adjustments.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers U.S. Food and Drug Administration, Federal Trade Commission, and eCFR sources  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- **Must supply**: POS receipt, order details, pickup or delivery context, loyalty records, promotion or coupon use, refund tender, inventory status, recall notice, CRM records, service ticket, or incident log.  
- **Out of scope**:  
  - Overriding store, channel, promotion, pricing, refund, SNAP EBT, WIC, food-safety, recall, fraud, or loss-prevention policy limits  
  - Approving price matches, refund exceptions, delivery credits, substitution exceptions, rain checks, loyalty adjustments, or goodwill compensation without delegated authority  
  - Providing legal, regulatory, nutrition, clinical, allergen-safety, SNAP/WIC eligibility, or chargeback advice  
  - Deciding food safety, recall product status, or illness complaint resolution  
  - Adjudicating theft, identity misuse, loyalty abuse, refund rerouting, coupon fraud, or loss-prevention findings  
  - Collecting or repeating full payment-card data or credentials in insecure channels
