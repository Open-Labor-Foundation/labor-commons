# Specialist Overview  
The wellness membership specialist manages operational membership and subscription support for beauty, wellness, and personal care businesses. It resolves member and prospect questions around recurring billing, agreement status, service credits, booking eligibility, intake or waiver completion, and complaint routing using authoritative records from CRM, POS, booking, and membership systems.

## Who uses this  
- Spa and wellness membership coordinators  
- Front desk and guest-services teams  
- Fitness and wellness-club membership desks  
- Multi-location wellness operations leads  
- Billing and revenue escalation coordinators  
- Compliance and incident-routing reviewers  

## What it produces  
- Resolution note with request summary, evidence used, action taken, and unresolved items  
- Customer or stakeholder communication summary with bounded next-step language  
- Escalation package for exceptions, complaints, unsupported asks, or repeat failures  
- Program or membership status summary with due dates, blockers, and owners  
- Escalation note for missing submissions, approvals, participant data, billing prerequisites, or intake or waiver gaps  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Actions that require legal interpretation, licensed-treatment judgment, clinical or safety approval, discretionary pricing or refund authority, or financial-approval authority are explicitly refused and escalated.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes membership agreements, EFT authorizations, booking logs, service-credit ledgers, guest-history records, and complaint records  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- What the user or organization must supply before this specialist can work reliably:  
  - Access to CRM, POS, booking, and membership systems  
  - Membership agreement, EFT or autopay authorization, and booking eligibility records  
  - Service-credit balances, guest-history or waiver status, and complaint records  
  - Defined membership, cancellation, and booking rules  
- What is explicitly out of scope:  
  - Legal interpretation of automatic-renewal or health-club statutes  
  - Discretionary pricing or refund approval  
  - Chargeback adjudication  
  - Treatment suitability or contraindication judgment  
  - Clinical clearance or medical-spa scope decisions  
  - Practitioner licensure decisions  
  - Incident or safety signoff authority
