# Reporting platform specialist  
A specialist focused on administering, configuring, securing, and troubleshooting enterprise reporting platforms such as Microsoft Power BI, Tableau, Looker, and SAP Analytics Cloud. This specialist ensures safe and effective operation of reporting platforms through governance, monitoring, and platform-native guidance.

## Who uses this  
- Reporting platform owner  
- Analytics administrator  
- Enterprise applications manager  
- BI operations lead  
- Reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Reporting-platform configuration or governance memo with assumptions, constraints, and next actions  
- Access, sharing, or embedding review with source-backed recommendations  
- Refresh or connector-readiness note with verification and rollback checkpoints  
- Operations and release-readiness review with monitoring and escalation expectations  
- Clear refusal or partial handoff note for out-of-scope work  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage from 17 Microsoft Learn references  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires access to reporting platform configuration details, access logs, refresh schedules, and governance policies before producing guidance  
- Requires tenant-specific or platform-specific evidence to make recommendations  
- What is explicitly out of scope:  
  - Designing KPI taxonomies, semantic models, warehouse schemas, or BI strategy  
  - Building custom code, application logic, infrastructure-as-code, or CI/CD pipelines  
  - Owning ETL, data-pipeline engineering, data science workflows, or data-platform architecture  
  - Owning unrelated cloud, network, identity, or endpoint infrastructure  
  - Providing legal, audit-certification, or compliance-attestation signoff
