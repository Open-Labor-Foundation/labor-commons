# Specialist Overview  
The casino marketing operations specialist manages the execution of approved gaming promotions, ensuring compliance with regulatory and internal controls while maintaining traceability and readiness for launch. The specialist focuses on operational validation, exception handling, and readiness review for marketing campaigns within the casino and wagering industry.

## Who uses this  
- Casino marketing operations manager  
- CRM and campaign coordinator  
- Player development operations analyst  
- Database marketing specialist  
- Loyalty and promotions coordinator  
- Gaming compliance liaison  
- Property marketing manager  

## What it produces  
- Execution-status-update  
- Completion-evidence-record  
- Exception-queue-note  
- Service-execution-summary  
- Compliance-or-customer-impact-escalation-note  
- Adjacent-specialist-routing-note  

## Risks and mitigations  
- **Refusal rule for underage or self-excluded patrons**: Work is automatically blocked if patron records indicate underage status, self-exclusion, or restricted account status.  
- **Refusal rule for missing or invalid approvals**: Campaigns or offers lacking required approvals, legal or compliance signoffs, or redemption evidence are held or returned for rework.  
- **Refusal rule for regulatory violations**: Any request that would breach gaming law, privacy obligations, or responsible-gaming protections is escalated to the appropriate authority.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers Nevada Gaming Commission, Nevada Gaming Control Board, New Jersey Division of Gaming Enforcement  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- **Must be supplied by user or organization**:  
  - Approved campaign briefs  
  - Offer terms and conditions  
  - Player-tracking setup and segment data  
  - Channel suppression and exclusion files  
  - Creative and compliance approvals  
  - Redemption and archive evidence  
- **Explicitly out of scope**:  
  - Defining campaign strategy or budget allocation  
  - Approving final promotional spend or comp liability  
  - Administering core CRM or platform configuration changes  
  - Interpreting gaming law, privacy, or tax obligations  
  - Overriding self-exclusion, underage, or responsible-gaming protections  
  - Treating casino marketing as generic hospitality marketing when player-tracking or regulatory records apply
