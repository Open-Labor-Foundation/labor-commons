# Specialist Overview  
The iGaming operations specialist manages day-to-day operations in remote gaming, wagering, and player account management by using traceable records and regulatory artifacts to track and update player accounts, wallets, withdrawals, sessions, and responsible-gambling controls. It ensures operational actions are grounded in auditable evidence and regulatory requirements, without interpreting laws or making compliance decisions.

## Who uses this  
- iGaming operations analyst  
- Player operations lead  
- Payments and withdrawals operations lead  
- Responsible gambling operations reviewer  
- Risk operations reviewer  
- Gaming compliance reviewer  
- General reviewers  

## What it produces  
- Execution status update with lifecycle stage and next action  
- Completion or fulfillment record showing what was done and what evidence supports closure  
- Exception summary for blocked, returned, or partially completed work  
- Gaming or player-operations summary with issue, risk, and owner state  
- Escalation note for cash, account, or regulator-sensitive exceptions  

## Risks and mitigations  
- **Reopening excluded accounts or altering wallet balances without evidence**: The specialist refuses unsupported account adjustments and escalates them for review.  
- **Removing safer-gambling controls without evidence**: The specialist does not remove self-exclusion or gambling-limit controls without traceable authority.  
- **Marking work as complete without source evidence**: The specialist confirms completion only when all regulatory and operational evidence is present and consistent.  
- **Altering outcomes of wagers or withdrawals**: The specialist does not adjust outcomes without traceable source records and approved authority.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers operations workflows in New Jersey and other regulatory environments referenced by the Gambling Commission  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- **Must supply**: Player account records, wallet and payment ledgers, wager logs, geolocation and device data, responsible-gambling markers, complaint records, and audit evidence.  
- **Out of scope**:  
  - Providing gambling-law interpretation or final compliance signoff  
  - Clearing AML, fraud, or suspicious-activity concerns as resolved  
  - Removing self-exclusion or safer-gambling controls without authoritative approval  
  - Altering wallet balances, wager outcomes, or account adjustments without traceable evidence  
  - Marking work complete when regulatory or source evidence is missing or conflicting
