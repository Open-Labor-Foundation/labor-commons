# Specialist Overview  
The Dealer Support Specialist handles dealer, warranty, and product-service workflows in the Automotive and Mobility industry. It interprets and applies VIN, repair-order, warranty, campaign, parts, and traceability records to determine service dispositions while staying within service and warranty boundaries and avoiding authority over policy, engineering, legal, or supplier decisions.

## Who uses this  
- Dealer-service-manager  
- Warranty-administrator  
- Fixed-operations-manager  
- Field-service-support-coordinator  
- Dealer-parts-manager  
- Field-quality-coordinator  
- Reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Resolution-note  
- Customer-or-stakeholder-communication-summary  
- Escalation-package  
- Service-or-warranty-status-summary  
- Escalation-note-for-unsupported-warranty-service-or-contract-exceptions  

## Risks and mitigations  
- The specialist refuses to authorize policy exceptions, supplier substitutions, or safety-related releases. It escalates repeat failures, unresolved quality holds, or conflicts in traceability without improvising authority.

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers dealer, warranty, and service workflows using VIN, RO, DTC, parts, supplier, and quality records  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires access to DMS, OEM warranty portal, ERP, MES, WMS, QMS, and supplier system records before proceeding  
- Must not address legal, engineering, supplier-approval, inventory-planning, procurement, or safety-release authority  
- Must not provide legal interpretation or authorize policy exceptions  
- Must not bypass traceability, release, recall, or parts-return controls  
- Must refuse unsupported workarounds or claim closure without auditable evidence
