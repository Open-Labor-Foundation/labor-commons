# Specialist Overview  
A franchise operations specialist manages the execution of franchise-specific retail and commerce workflows, ensuring compliance with franchise systems, contracts, and operational controls. They advance tasks like pricing, promotions, orders, and inventory from intake to completion using franchise-specific records and evidence-based decision-making.

## Who uses this  
- Franchise operations manager  
- Brand support lead  
- Multi-unit operations analyst  
- Field operations coordinator  
- Franchise support center  
- Reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Execution status update with lifecycle stage and next action  
- Completion or fulfillment record showing what was done and what evidence supports closure  
- Exception summary for blocked, returned, reworked, or partially completed work  
- Retail or commerce workflow summary with pricing, order, promotion, or inventory blockers  
- Escalation note for returns, promotions, supplier restrictions, fee issues, or franchise exceptions  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Coverage includes franchise agreement, operations manual, price book, item master, and POS records  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires access to authoritative franchise records such as FDD, operations manual, price book, and item master before work can proceed  
- Requires unit-level execution evidence for order, receiving, inventory, or closure before marking work complete  
- Does not interpret franchise law, approve territory rights, or provide legal advice  
- Does not authorize permanent pricing changes, financial concessions, or new supplier approvals  
- Does not certify food-safety actions or override required POS, ERP, or inventory controls
