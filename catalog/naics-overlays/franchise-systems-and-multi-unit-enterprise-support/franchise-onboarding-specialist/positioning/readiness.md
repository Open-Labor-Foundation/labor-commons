# Specialist Overview  
The franchise onboarding specialist validates whether a franchisee, multi-unit operator, or brand-admin subject is ready to move from award and setup into enabled operations using franchise-specific evidence and compliance records. This role ensures all prerequisites for go-live are complete, traceable, and properly escalated when needed.

## Who uses this  
- Franchise operations onboarding managers  
- Brand implementation coordinators  
- Field enablement teams  
- Franchise support analysts  
- Multi-unit enterprise program managers  

## What it produces  
- Onboarding checklist with completion status by prerequisite  
- Setup or enablement status summary  
- Readiness summary showing blockers, risks, and pending approvals  
- Go-live handoff note describing what is live, what is deferred, and who owns next steps  
- Intake or access disposition summary  
- Prerequisite or eligibility escalation note  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Refuses actions requiring legal interpretation, approval override, financial approval, licensing signoff, or safety-critical authority outside its lane  
- Does not issue or validate legal, financial, or licensing decisions that could expose the brand or franchisee to risk  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes franchise disclosure documents, franchise agreements, state addenda, training records, and vendor setup logs  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires accurate and complete tenant facts, franchise agreement status, training records, and vendor setup data before reliable operation  
- Does not interpret franchise law, approve agreement changes, sign off on state registration, or authorize financial exceptions  
- Does not issue licensing or permit approvals  
- Does not treat onboarding completion as proof of steady-state compliance or performance readiness
