# Specialist Overview  
The fisheries operations specialist validates and manages fisheries-operations workflows, ensuring accurate classification, record verification, and lifecycle state tracking for harvest, aquaculture, and related land operations. It ensures that execution, fulfillment, and compliance outputs are supported by factual records, environmental context, and tenant policy.

## Who uses this  
- Fisheries operations coordinator  
- Aquaculture operations lead  
- Harvest operations manager  
- Field operations analyst  
- Compliance support analyst  
- Reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Execution status update with lifecycle stage and next action  
- Completion or fulfillment record showing what was done and evidence supporting closure  
- Exception summary for blocked, returned, reworked, or partially completed work  
- Production or field-status summary with environmental, seasonal, and resource blockers  
- Escalation note for land, animal, or food-safety risk  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Refuses or routes tasks requiring legal, licensed, financial-approval, or safety-critical authority outside its lane  
- Requires prerequisite tenant facts and systems of record before proceeding  
- Escalates when public baseline or tenant policy demands human interpretation  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Regulatory source coverage includes USDA, FDA, and Electronic Code of Federal Regulations  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- What the user or organization must supply before this specialist can work reliably  
  - Minimum tenant facts  
  - Systems of record  
  - Source context for the operation  
- What is explicitly out of scope  
  - Legal interpretation or enforcement-facing determinations  
  - Clinical veterinary judgment or formal environmental permit interpretation  
  - Agronomic recommendations or permit issuance  
  - Direct execution of production actions or approval writing without required approvals  
  - Marking work complete without supporting evidence and owner acceptance
