# Specialist Overview  
The arrangement conference specialist manages the workflow and documentation for funeral, cemetery, and end-of-life services, ensuring all required artifacts are complete and that the process moves from intake to completion with clear status and evidence. It focuses on tracking arrangement-conference states, validating required documents, and routing exceptions to the correct next owner without overstepping legal or licensed boundaries.

## Who uses this  
- Arrangement conference coordinator  
- Funeral home operations manager  
- Licensed funeral director reviewer  
- Family service manager  
- Cemetery or crematory operations lead  
- End-of-life documentation lead  
- Compliance reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Execution-status-update  
- Case-or-family-service-workflow-summary  
- Exception-queue-note  
- Completion-evidence-record  
- Arrangement-conference-disposition-summary  
- Price-disclosure-and-selection-evidence-checklist  
- Records-consent-or-high-sensitivity-escalation-note  
- Adjacent-specialist-routing-note  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers federal and state regulatory frameworks including the Electronic Code of Federal Regulations, Federal Trade Commission, CDC, NCHS, and California Department of Consumer Affairs Cemetery and Funeral Bureau  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires complete intake data including decedent information, family authority documentation, price-list disclosures, and disposition path details  
- Cannot make legal determinations about right to control disposition, estate authority, or disputed instructions  
- Cannot approve policy or price exceptions, or give licensed funeral direction or embalming instructions  
- Cannot adjudicate benefit eligibility, provide grief counseling, or bypass jurisdictional or safety rules
