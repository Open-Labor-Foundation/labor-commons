# Deployment Package

## Runtime and Deployment Profile

- Runtime strategy: `specification-based`
- Target delivery level: `validated`
- Runtime promotion condition: promote to retained-implementation only after explicit governance signoff, repeated runtime demand, and stable validation history.
- Execution target: `commons-crew`

## Materialization Requirements

### Required Task and Boundary Metadata

- `manifest.yaml` with explicit production-accounting boundaries, refusal triggers, adjacent-owner routing, and tenant fact requirements.
- `evaluation/functionality-map.json` covering shared + revenue-billing-and-financial-reconciliation + cross-industry abilities.
- `evaluation/research-summary.json` with source classes, workflow stages, artifacts, systems of record, decision boundaries, domain failure modes, and unresolved ambiguity posture.
- `evaluation/results.json` and `readiness/evidence.json` showing complete scenario coverage, validated gates, and human verification.

### Runtime Assumptions

- Tenant supplies production type, jurisdiction, signatory package, payroll-service model, incentive-program election, chart-of-accounts mapping, approval thresholds, and rights-holder billing posture.
- Production accounting ledger, payroll, AP, cash-management, document-retrieval, budgeting, scheduling, and incentive-support records are retrievable at runtime.
- Tenant provides current escalation maps for controller, payroll tax, labor relations, legal or business affairs, residuals, and tax-incentive owners.
- Broadcast, feature, episodic, commercial, live, remote, and streamer production distinctions are available whenever they materially change the paperwork, labor, billing, or incentive workflow.

### Required Retrieval Dependencies

- Production title, episode or unit, production type, company code, jurisdiction, signatory package, and reporting period.
- Account coding map, cost-report cadence, budget version, cost-ledger detail, approval chain, and close checklist.
- Deal memos, start packets, timecards, Exhibit G reports, payroll registers, fringe reports, loan-out support, invoices, purchase orders, petty-cash envelopes, DPRs, and call sheets.
- Cash books, bank statements, wire or ACH confirmations, p-card logs, qualified-spend ledgers, incentive election status, and current audit requests.

### Refusal and Return Conditions

- Missing tenant facts, unavailable system-of-record access, or stale or contradictory production paperwork, payroll, AP, bank, or incentive evidence.
- Requests for controller approval, accounting policy, payroll-tax treatment, labor-contract interpretation, legal advice, residuals determination, or credit certification.
- Unresolved signatory conflict, production-type ambiguity, incentive-rule conflict, or unsupported override pressure that materially changes the answer.
- Public baseline and tenant policy conflict without explicit precedence resolution.

## Rollback and Recovery

- Pause tenant routing for this lane if repeated unsupported reclassifications, false posting confidence, incentive misclassification, or production-type flattening is detected.
- Preserve all posting summaries, reconciliation notes, evidence maps, and escalation packets for post-rollback review.
- Re-enable only after source-of-truth retrieval, signatory-package routing, incentive classification controls, and tenant policy precedence are reconfirmed.

## retained-implementation Promotion Plan

- Keep `specification-based` execution until a separate retained-implementation package is explicitly approved and committed.
- Promote only after repeated production usage shows this production-accounting lane is common and stable enough to justify a maintained build with governed connectors.
- Promotion requires formal validation signoff, updated readiness evidence, and a new PR that records the retained-implementation runtime separately from this spec pack.
