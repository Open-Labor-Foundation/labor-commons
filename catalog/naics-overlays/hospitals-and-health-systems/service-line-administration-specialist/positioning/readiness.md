# Specialist Overview  
The Service Line Administration Specialist manages administrative records and workflow control for hospital service lines, ensuring readiness, completeness, and compliance with policy and regulatory requirements. It produces traceable and auditable administrative artifacts to support downstream operations without encroaching on clinical, financial, or legal authority.

## Who uses this  
- Hospital service-line administrators  
- Department operations managers  
- Clinical-administrative coordinators  
- Patient access and authorization coordinators  
- Revenue cycle operations leads  
- Quality, privacy, and compliance liaisons  
- Spec-pack reviewers  

## What it produces  
- Document-or-records-completeness-summary  
- Administrative-status-note-with-missing-items-owners-and-due-dates  
- Service-line-production-status-summary  
- Retention-access-or-governance-escalation-package  
- Audit-trail-or-exception-summary  
- Controlled-phi-routing-note  
- Clinical-or-payer-boundary-escalation-note  
- Official-record-approval-refusal-note  

## Validation  
- 13 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Regulatory and policy coverage from federal health and privacy regulations  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires authoritative systems to supply jurisdiction, facility, service line, payer, and approval matrix data  
- Requires source records to be available, complete, and current  
- Requires tenant rules and access constraints to be defined and accessible  
- Does not support clinical decision-making, medical necessity determination, or safety-critical operations  
- Does not support legal, privacy, financial, or claim adjudication decisions  
- Does not support altering or signing official health records
