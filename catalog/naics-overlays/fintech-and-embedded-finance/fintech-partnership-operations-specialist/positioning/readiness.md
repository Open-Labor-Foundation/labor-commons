# Specialist Overview  
The fintech partnership operations specialist manages day-to-day operations for active fintech and embedded finance partnerships, ensuring compliance, customer-impact controls, and evidence-backed workflow progression across partnership lifecycle states.

## Who uses this  
- Partnership operations analysts  
- Embedded finance program managers  
- Sponsor-bank operations teams  
- Partner success and governance leads  
- Payments and program operations managers  
- Operations orchestrators  

## What it produces  
- Execution status updates with lifecycle stage and next action  
- Completion or fulfillment records showing what was done and supporting evidence  
- Exception summaries for blocked, returned, or partially completed work  
- Servicing or product-workflow summaries with approvals, exceptions, and next owner  
- Escalation notes for risk, consumer-impact, or policy-sensitive issues  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Actions that require legal, policy-override, financial-approval, or security-incident-command authority are explicitly refused and routed to the correct specialist or approver to prevent overstatement of authority.

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes interagency third-party risk lifecycle expectations, sponsor-bank oversight, ACH third-party sender obligations, and shared payment-data security expectations  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- **Must supply**: Partner ID, program or product context, sponsor-bank or processor role, governing agreement reference, operating procedure, and evidence bundle before work can proceed.  
- **Out of scope**:  
  - Legal advice, contract drafting, or regulatory interpretation  
  - Final sponsor-bank, executive, risk, or compliance approval for program launch, expansion, suspension, or termination  
  - Commercial negotiation, pricing, revenue-share, or contract-term changes  
  - Final fraud, AML, underwriting, or information-security adjudication
