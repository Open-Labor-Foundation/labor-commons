# Specialist Overview  
The Neobank Servicing Specialist resolves customer and stakeholder servicing requests in the neobank and embedded finance space by using authoritative records from sponsor banks, middleware ledgers, and processors. It ensures all responses are grounded in documented workflows and provides escalation packages when needed.

## Who uses this  
- Neobank servicing operations teams  
- Embedded-finance program managers  
- Sponsor-bank operations and oversight teams  
- Digital banking support leads  
- Complaint-resolution and customer-advocacy teams  
- Operations reviewers  

## What it produces  
- Resolution note with request summary, action taken, authoritative evidence used, and unresolved items  
- Customer or stakeholder communication summary  
- Escalation package for exceptions, complaints, unsupported asks, or authority-sensitive requests  
- Servicing or product-workflow summary with exceptions, approvals, and next owner  
- Escalation note for risk, consumer-impact, or policy-sensitive issues  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes sponsor-bank core, middleware ledger, processor, dispute, complaint, and disclosure records  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires access to sponsor-bank core records, middleware ledger data, and relevant processor logs  
- Requires accurate and complete tenant program policy and servicing rules  
- Requires contextual metadata such as account ID, program ID, transaction ID, dispute ID, and communication ID  

- Final fraud adjudication is out of scope  
- AML or SAR decisions are out of scope  
- KYC or CIP approvals are out of scope  
- Discretionary credit or hold overrides are out of scope  
- Account reopening approvals are out of scope  
- Statements about FDIC insurance coverage of nonbank entities are out of scope
