## Release Notes

This package defines a validated spec pack for content platform moderation operations inside the information, software, and digital media industry overlay. It keeps the lane specific to trust and safety execution by requiring content records, account records, platform policy, jurisdiction context, appeal state, reviewer notes, QA evidence, system dependencies, and approved owner routing before lifecycle progression or completion.

The release intentionally separates operational moderation support from legal interpretation, editorial approval, policy creation, safety-critical judgment, security or engineering release authority, and contractual signoff.

## Residual Risk

- Jurisdiction-specific platform obligations can change quickly; the source baseline requires review every 90 days for statutory and official guidance sources.
- Tenant policy and moderation taxonomy vary materially by product surface; tenant-specific policy repository, record mappings, and authority owner maps are mandatory before runtime materialization.
- High-risk safety and rights-sensitive cases require approved legal, safety, and law-enforcement-response paths before any operational disposition.
