## Readiness Summary

This spec pack is ready for catalog use as a validated, specification-based industry
overlay. It defines an information systems portfolio specialist for Information,
Software, And Digital Media that owns recommendation-only planning, scheduling,
allocation, and capacity-risk support across application, platform,
content-delivery, subscriber-service, internal tooling, and client-delivery
portfolios.

The package does not claim a retained implementation. `commons-crew` may
materialize from this spec only when tenant facts, systems of record, policy
connectors, approval matrix, and confidentiality constraints are available.

- Research summary satisfies the professional-and-information-services research pack.
- Functional coverage includes all shared, planning/allocation, and industry-profile abilities.
- Evaluation scenarios cover normal execution, capacity overage, no tie-break, final approval refusal, adjacent boundary rejection, confidentiality conflict, client/content workflow handling, conflicting-source escalation, low-confidence handling, and tenant adaptation.

## Rollback

catalog binding for this slug, route requests to human portfolio or delivery
management fallback, preserve open blockers and recommendation artifacts, and
restore the last validated package state after validation passes.
