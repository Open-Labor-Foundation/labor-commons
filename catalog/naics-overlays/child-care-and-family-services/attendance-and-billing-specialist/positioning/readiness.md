# Specialist Overview  
The attendance and billing specialist transforms child-care attendance, service authorization, family fee, subsidy, and funded-enrollment records into traceable billing-readiness, posting, reconciliation, and financial exception outputs. It operates within the child care and family services industry overlay, ensuring billing decisions are evidence-backed and scoped to attendance, fee, and remittance records without assuming roles in accounting, compliance, or legal authority.

## Who uses this  
- Attendance-and-billing-coordinator  
- Child-care-center-director  
- Family-services-or-ERSEA-operations-staff  
- Subsidy-billing-team  
- Reconciliation-analyst  
- Reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Billing, invoice, claim, or posting summary with source traceability  
- Reconciliation note showing matched items, exceptions, denials, and unresolved variances  
- Financial exception escalation for unsupported adjustments, disputes, write-offs, or denials  
- Billing or financial reconciliation summary with root-cause categories  
- Revenue exception escalation note  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes HeadStart.gov / Administration for Children and Families (10 times cited)  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- What the user or organization must supply before this specialist can work reliably  
  - Valid and complete attendance records  
  - Service authorization spans  
  - Family fee and co-payment support details  
  - Subsidy or voucher posting readiness indicators  
  - Tenant billing rules and public-source guidance  
- What is explicitly out of scope  
  - Determining tax treatment  
  - Creating formal accounting entries  
  - Awarding subsidy eligibility  
  - Authorizing write-offs, refunds, or credit memos  
  - Interpreting legal or licensing waivers  
  - Overriding program-policy or financial approvals  
  - Resolving conflicts between public guidance and tenant/jurisdiction rules without human review
