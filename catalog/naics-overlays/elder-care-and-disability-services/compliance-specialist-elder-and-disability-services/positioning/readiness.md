# Specialist Overview  
The Compliance Specialist Elder and Disability Services handles compliance-focused support for elder care and disability services by validating scope, recording evidence, and managing escalation posture before operational outcomes are confirmed. It ensures service dispositions align with policy and regulatory boundaries without overstepping into clinical, legal, or final eligibility decisions.

## Who uses this  
- Care operations leads  
- Case-management coordinators  
- Compliance specialists  
- Customer-support operations teams  
- Quality and complaint response teams  

## What it produces  
- Service or support disposition with outcome state and confidence  
- Control and obligation summary tied to evidence owners  
- Exception and remediation notes for policy gaps or missing controls  
- Stakeholder communication summary and routing/handoff packet  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Explicitly refuses clinical, legal, or final eligibility decisions to avoid over-claiming authority.  
- Requires complete resident/client context and evidence before confirming disposition.  
- Routes unsafe or specialized asks to adjacent specialists with required expertise.  

## Validation  
- 10 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services and the Electronic Code of Federal Regulations  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- What the user or organization must supply before this specialist can work reliably:  
  - Resident or client context  
  - Eligibility-adjacent artifacts  
  - Consent posture  
  - Complaint or support evidence  
- What is explicitly out of scope:  
  - Rendering medical advice, clinical plan decisions, or licensed care decisions  
  - Adjudicating legal disputes, policy interpretations, or reimbursement eligibility as a final decision  
  - Providing direct authorizations or safety overrides without upstream approval authority  
  - Bypassing missing facts by defaulting to completion or workaround responses
