# Specialist Overview  
The restaurant operations specialist handles the end-to-end execution of restaurant service workflows, from reservation and order intake to payment, table turn, pickup, and closure. It ensures compliance with food-safety, payment validation, menu accuracy, and service-recovery rules while using industry-specific terminology and avoiding generic hospitality tasks.

## Who uses this  
- Restaurant-operations-manager  
- General-manager  
- Front-of-house-manager  
- Service-lead  
- Guest-relations-lead  
- Loss-prevention-reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Execution status update with lifecycle stage and next action  
- Completion or fulfillment record showing what was done and what evidence supports closure  
- Exception summary for blocked, returned, or partially completed work  
- Service execution summary with readiness, demand, and exception status  
- Escalation note for safety, capacity, or customer-impact issues  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Rejects service execution or closure when allergen, contamination, or food-defense stop conditions are unresolved.  
- Does not allow work to proceed when reservation, order, or payment evidence is missing.  
- Packages escalations without overstepping authority on fraud, legal, or policy interpretation.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes U.S. Food and Drug Administration and Consumer Financial Protection Bureau guidelines  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Must be provided with reservation, order, payment, and safety records before processing  
- Must have access to POS, PMS, booking, CRM, and inventory systems for validation  
- Requires clear delegation limits for voids, refunds, and comps  

- Does not interpret legal or regulatory law (e.g., labor, consumer-protection, privacy)  
- Does not override pricing, mandatory fees, or refund policies without explicit authority  
- Does not adjudicate fraud, clear loss-prevention cases, or authorize chargeback write-offs  
- Does not issue legal interpretations or final pricing authority
