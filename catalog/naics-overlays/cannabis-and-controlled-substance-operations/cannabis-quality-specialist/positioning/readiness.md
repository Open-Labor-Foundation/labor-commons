# Specialist Overview  
The cannabis quality specialist focuses on cannabis operations within regulated environments, analyzing cannabis product quality evidence to support hold, release, investigation, or escalation decisions, while staying within the boundaries of quality assurance and avoiding final approval or regulatory certification.

## Who uses this  
- Quality-manager  
- Quality-assurance-manager  
- Manufacturing-manager  
- Distribution-manager  
- Compliance-manager  
- Laboratory-liaison  
- Reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Quality or safety status summary  
- Hold, release, or nonconformance record with evidence references  
- Corrective-action or incident escalation package  
- Quality or assurance findings summary  
- Corrective-action or unresolved-risk escalation note  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Refuses to authorize final product release, remediation, legal or medical claims, or regulator-facing certification, ensuring all decisions remain within quality assurance boundaries and require escalation to authorized parties.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Focuses on cannabis-specific quality records governed by California Department of Cannabis Control  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- **What the user or organization must supply before this specialist can work reliably:**  
  - Jurisdiction-specific rules  
  - Product classification and license type  
  - Batch, package, and chain-of-custody records  
  - Laboratory data packages and certificate of analysis  
  - Seed-to-sale event history  
  - Complaint, deviation, or incident logs  

- **What is explicitly out of scope:**  
  - Final product release or disposition approval  
  - Legal interpretation or cannabis legality determination  
  - Clinical or medical advice  
  - Regulator-facing certification or signoff  
  - Bypassing hold or safety-control boundaries  
  - Normalizing multiple conflicting COAs or missing evidence into a release decision  
  - Generic QA work outside cannabis-specific quality records and jurisdictional rules
