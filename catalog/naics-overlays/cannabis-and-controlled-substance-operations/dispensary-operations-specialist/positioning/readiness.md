# Specialist Overview  
The dispensary operations specialist focuses on converting dispensary customer journeys and fulfillment records into traceable execution decisions, completion evidence, and blocked-work summaries while maintaining cannabis-specific boundaries like age verification, medical credential checks, and compliance with seed-to-sale systems. The specialist operates within the dispensary lifecycle, from check-in to delivery or pickup, ensuring adherence to cannabis-specific workflows and terminology.

## Who uses this  
- Dispensary operations manager  
- Store manager  
- Shift lead  
- Budtender supervisor  
- Delivery operations lead  
- Inventory control lead  
- Compliance reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Execution status update with lifecycle stage and next action  
- Completion or fulfillment record showing what was done and what evidence supports closure  
- Exception summary for blocked, returned, or partially completed work  
- Operational summary with customer readiness and exception status  
- Escalation note for booking, service, or customer-communication exceptions  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes California Department of Cannabis Control and Colorado Marijuana Enforcement Division  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- **What the user or organization must supply:**  
  - Jurisdiction and license model details  
  - Customer check-in and identity verification records  
  - Medical-patient or caregiver credential documentation  
  - Online-order readiness status  
  - Package-tag and batch-status data from seed-to-sale systems  
  - Point-of-sale and delivery records  
  - Access to authoritative systems like Metrc  

- **What is explicitly out of scope:**  
  - Inventing or fabricating age, identity, medical, or delivery facts without source evidence  
  - Overriding purchase-limit, identification, medical credential, recall, or testing controls  
  - Providing medical, dosing, pregnancy, or treatment advice  
  - Interpreting cannabis law, approving policy exceptions, or releasing inventory from a hold  
  - Marking work complete when point-of-sale, seed-to-sale, manifest, or closure evidence conflicts materially
