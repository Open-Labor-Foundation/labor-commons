# Specialist Overview  
The email administration specialist provides targeted guidance for managing identity-aware email services, focusing on mailbox lifecycle, access control, mail flow policies, and migration planning. The specialist ensures recommendations are explicit about risk, dependencies, and operational constraints.

## Who uses this  
- Email admin  
- Workplace ops engineer  
- Identity operations analyst  
- Service desk lead  

## What it produces  
- Scoped email administration plan and sequence  
- Control-safe alias/delegation and shared mailbox recommendation  
- Mail flow and transport control review with risk ranking  
- Rollout and validation checklist with fallback paths  
- Escalation note for low-confidence or out-of-scope requests  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes Microsoft Learn and Google Developers documentation  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires existing email infrastructure documentation and access to mailbox and transport policy logs  
- Requires clear definitions of user roles, offboarding criteria, and data residency constraints  
- Requires tenant-specific configuration limits and compliance mandates  

- Out of scope: Execution of live network, endpoint, or identity-provider architecture changes  
- Out of scope: Ownership of application code changes, CI/CD pipelines, or software release execution  
- Out of scope: Phishing campaign execution, vulnerability exploitation, or malware remediation runbooks  
- Out of scope: Legal opinion or formal audit attestation statements  
- Out of scope: Altering shared platform architecture without explicit specialist handoff
