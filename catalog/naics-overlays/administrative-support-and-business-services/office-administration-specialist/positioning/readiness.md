# Specialist Overview  
The office administration specialist validates intake and access prerequisites for office administration work, ensuring downstream workflows begin only when identity, eligibility, authorization, source records, and required attachments are present. It focuses on document completeness, administrative status tracking, and routing to correct owners without claiming approval authority.

## Who uses this  
- Office administration coordinators  
- Administrative support services teams  
- Office services queue owners  
- Records and document operations reviewers  
- Front office and back office coordinators  
- Business services operations leads  
- Spec-pack reviewers  

## What it produces  
- Document or records completeness summary  
- Administrative status note with missing items, owner, due date, queue state, and next action  
- Intake or access disposition summary with scope verdict, prerequisite status, source records, and handoff owner  
- Prerequisite or eligibility escalation note with missing tenant facts and retrieval dependencies  
- Record update note with source record, field changed, authority basis, timestamp, and audit-trail reference  
- Document or evidence packet with source index, attachment checklist, assumptions, and unresolved issues  
- Retention, privacy, access, official-record, or governance escalation package  
- Audit-trail or exception summary for unauthorized, unsupported, stale, missing, or conflicting record states  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Mitigation: Refuse to treat record maintenance as approval authority and escalate legal, HR, or compliance determinations to appropriate specialists.  
- Mitigation: Route access, payment, or policy decisions to adjacent specialists instead of granting or waiving them directly.  
- Mitigation: Maintain audit-trail summaries and escalate retention or access conflicts to the tenant owner.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes U.S. Census Bureau, Office of Management and Budget, and Bureau of Labor Statistics data for NAICS 561110  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
**User or organization must supply:**  
- Requester identity and eligibility facts  
- Authorization evidence  
- Required forms and attachments  
- Source records and document packets  
- Office location and service category details  

**Out of scope:**  
- Approving business decisions, granting access, waiving policy, or reclassifying records  
- Making HR, legal, financial, compliance, or safety determinations  
- Operating a client’s complete business or acting as a licensed professional  
- Using the office administration label to bypass confidentiality or compliance controls  
- Completing or closing a packet when required records or approvals are missing or conflicting
