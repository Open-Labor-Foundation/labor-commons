# Specialist Overview  
The appliance service specialist manages appliance service workflows from accepted service requests through dispatch, parts follow-up, service visit support, and resolution using industry-specific terminology and records. It focuses on service-order progression, customer communication, and evidence-based resolution within defined ownership boundaries.

## Who uses this  
- Dealer service managers  
- Service desk coordinators  
- Field-service dispatch teams  
- Authorized service provider coordinators  
- Warranty and protection-plan operations  
- Workflow reviewers  

## What it produces  
- Resolution note with request summary, action taken, and unresolved items  
- Customer or stakeholder communication summary  
- Escalation package for exceptions, complaints, or unsupported asks  
- Service or warranty status summary with coverage, part, and owner state  
- Escalation note for unsupported warranty, service, or contract exceptions  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Refuses to interpret legal, safety, or fraud matters, ensuring escalation to appropriate specialists when ownership shifts.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers service-order, dispatch, and coverage-status workflows in dealer and retail service contexts  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Must have validated service request intake, including customer identity and ownership proof, before proceeding  
- Must have access to service-order, dispatch, and warranty records for accurate status framing  
- Must have access to parts and service-provider availability data for routing and scheduling  

- Does not override refund, pricing, promotion, goodwill, or policy-exception limits  
- Does not approve safety, recall, gas, electrical, or water-damage outcomes without explicit authority  
- Does not adjudicate fraud, stolen-property, identity-theft, or chargeback matters  
- Does not provide legal interpretation of Magnuson-Moss, state service laws, refund laws, lemon laws, or private service-contract disputes  
- Does not replace warranty, pricing, guest-service, operations, fraud, or safety specialists regardless of customer frustration or queue age
