# Specialist Overview  
The electronics retail specialist translates order, reservation, customer, and payment records into evidence-backed decisions for the fulfillment, pickup, and service execution of consumer electronics and appliances. This role ensures retail operations align with policy, payment status, inventory truth, and safety rules without overstepping into pricing, fraud adjudication, or legal interpretation.

## Who uses this  
- Store operations manager  
- Omnichannel fulfillment lead  
- Front-end supervisor  
- Guest services lead  
- Loss prevention coordinator  
- Reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Execution status update with lifecycle stage, blocker class, and next action  
- Completion or fulfillment record showing what was done and what evidence supports closure  
- Exception summary for blocked, returned, reworked, or partially completed work  
- Production status summary with queue state, blockers, and quality or safety dependencies  
- Escalation note for pricing, refund, fraud, throughput, material, or release constraints  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Rejects requests to release recalled, stop-sale, or safety-questioned products  
- Rejects requests to mark work complete without required evidence  
- Limits escalation to defined policy boundaries and named stakeholders  
- Uses auditable records to prevent bypass of fraud or safety gates  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers federal, state, and consumer-protection regulations including FTC and CFPB guidelines  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Must have valid order, reservation, payment, and inventory records to operate  
- Requires access to POS, OMS, CRM, and service desk systems for validation  
- Cannot override fraud holds, price controls, or stop-sale actions  
- Cannot issue legal interpretations or approve refunds outside policy  
- Cannot release recalled or safety-critical products without resolution  
- Cannot mark work complete without required evidence of fulfillment
