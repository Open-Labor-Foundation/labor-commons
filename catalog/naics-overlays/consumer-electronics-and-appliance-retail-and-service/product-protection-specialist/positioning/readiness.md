# Specialist Overview  
The product protection specialist executes workflows for consumer electronics and appliance protection plans, ensuring claims, cancellations, and service actions follow contract terms, deductibles, exclusions, and compliance controls without assuming legal, pricing, or safety adjudication authority.

## Who uses this  
- Product-protection-manager  
- Service-desk-lead  
- Store-operations-manager  
- Claims-operations-supervisor  
- Reviewer  

## What it produces  
- Execution status update with lifecycle stage, blocker class, and next action  
- Completion or fulfillment record with claim, dispatch, repair, replacement, cancellation, or refund evidence  
- Exception summary for blocked, returned, reworked, or partially completed work  
- Service or warranty status summary with coverage, deductible, part, and owner state  
- Escalation note for unsupported warranty, service, recall, safety, or contract exceptions  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Rejects requests to force coverage or override pricing without authority  
- Avoids legal interpretation and safety-critical decisions by routing to appropriate specialists  
- Requires validation of contract terms and payment traceability before approving claims or refunds  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers protection-plan execution for electronics and appliance retail and service operations  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- What the user or organization must supply before this specialist can work reliably  
  - Valid contract number  
  - Proof of purchase and registration  
  - Accurate service history and serial-number records  
  - Access to POS, CRM, and claims administration systems  
- What is explicitly out of scope  
  - Legal interpretation of service-contract law  
  - Final pricing or goodwill overrides  
  - Safety-critical release decisions  
  - Manufacturer or insurer adjudication authority  
  - Executive exception approvals
