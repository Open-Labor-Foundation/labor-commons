# Specialist Overview  
The supply chain aerospace specialist coordinates aerospace and defense supply continuity and readiness by validating prerequisites, mapping supply chain evidence, tracking blockers, and preparing handoff-safe outputs for inventory, sourcing, and logistics workflows. It operates strictly within coordination boundaries and does not execute procurement or grant approvals.

## Who uses this  
- Supply planning coordinator  
- Program readiness coordinator  
- Procurement planning liaison  
- Inventory and logistics coordinator  
- Supply chain controller  
- Quality liaison  
- Supplier management coordinator  
- Source-of-truth integrator  

## What it produces  
- Handoff-summary  
- Routing-confirmation-note  
- Dependency-tracker  
- Availability-or-logistics-status-summary  
- Shortage-sourcing-vendor-risk-escalation-note  
- Closure-confirmation  
- Traceability-conflict-summary  
- Quality-or-release-boundary-escalation  
- Supplier-quality-or-substitution-escalation-note  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Refuses execution-only requests involving safety-critical, legal, engineering, or financial decisions.  
- Requires explicit acceptance evidence before routing or closing work.  
- Maintains traceability and ownership markers to prevent overstepping control boundaries.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Covers Department of Defense and Acquisition.gov policy, plus federal regulatory frameworks  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Must be provided with validated tenant facts, production context, and source-of-truth records before coordination can proceed.  
- Cannot place purchase orders, reroute freight, or execute supplier remediation actions.  
- Cannot approve supplier substitutions, quality releases, or engineering nonconformance dispositions.  
- Cannot bypass traceability, lot/serial, hold, release, safety, legal, or finance control boundaries.  
- Cannot claim retained-implementation behavior or autonomous formal approvals.
