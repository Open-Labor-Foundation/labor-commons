# Specialist Overview  
The data quality specialist focuses on ensuring the trustworthiness and reliability of datasets and data products by managing data quality dimensions such as completeness, validity, and consistency. They design validation rules, assess risks, and create remediation plans tailored to analytics, warehouse, and AI-adjacent workflows.

## Who uses this  
- Data engineer  
- Analytics engineer  
- Data steward  
- Reviewer  
- Platform owner  

## What it produces  
- Data quality assessment with explicit in-scope diagnosis and assumptions  
- Prioritized rule/test plan with thresholds and monitoring checkpoints  
- Incident triage and remediation runbook with rollback-safe verification steps  
- Boundary-safe refusal and orchestrator handoff for adjacent specialty requests  
- Confidence-gated escalation memo when evidence is incomplete or conflicting  

## Risks and mitigations  
- Refuses to handle governance policy authoring, ETL/ELT implementation, or model training unless explicitly required to explain data quality failures, ensuring work stays within technical quality boundaries.  
- Avoids ownership of dashboards, KPIs, or infrastructure tasks, preventing misalignment with core data quality responsibilities.  

## Validation  
- 12 evaluation scenarios, pass rate 1  
- Source coverage includes Microsoft Learn, Amazon Web Services, and Google Cloud  
- Human verification and functional audit completed  

## Limitations  
- Requires dataset metadata, profiling evidence, and lineage information to operate effectively  
- Does not own end-to-end ETL or ELT implementation, platform migration, or warehouse performance tuning  
- Does not author enterprise governance policy, legal/privacy positions, or compliance attestations  
- Does not define BI dashboards, KPI semantics, or business interpretation  
- Does not own machine-learning model training, feature engineering, or model-operations decisions  
- Does not perform unrelated infrastructure, networking, security architecture, or product strategy work
