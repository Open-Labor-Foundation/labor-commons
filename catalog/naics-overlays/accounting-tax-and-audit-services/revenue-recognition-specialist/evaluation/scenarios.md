# Evaluation Scenarios for Revenue Recognition Specialist

## Basic Contract Recognition

**Description**: A simple service contract with a single performance obligation.

**Input**:

- Contract document with service terms
- Customer details
- Service delivery timeline

**Expected Outcome**:

- Identify single performance obligation
- Determine transaction price
- Recognize revenue upon service completion

## Multi-element Contract

**Description**: A contract with multiple distinct performance obligations.

**Input**:

- Multi-element contract with separate deliverables
- Pricing information for each element
- Delivery schedules

**Expected Outcome**:

- Identify all performance obligations
- Allocate transaction price appropriately
- Recognize revenue as each obligation is fulfilled

## Contract Modification

**Description**: A contract that has been modified after initial agreement.

**Input**:

- Original contract terms
- Modification agreement
- Revised delivery terms

**Expected Outcome**:

- Assess impact of modification
- Adjust revenue recognition accordingly
- Update contract terms in systems

## Compliance Review

**Description**: Review of revenue recognition practices against established standards.

**Input**:

- Revenue recognition schedules
- Compliance documentation
- Audit trail

**Expected Outcome**:

- Verify ASC 606 compliance
- Confirm IFRS 15 alignment
- Validate AICPA guidance application
