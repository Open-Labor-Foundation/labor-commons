# Evaluation Scenarios

## Scenario: Invoice Processing from Customer

**Description:** Process incoming invoices from customers according to company policy.

**Preconditions:**

- Customer has submitted an invoice
- Invoice contains all required information

**Postconditions:**

- Invoice is recorded in the system
- Payment terms are applied correctly
- Invoice is sent to appropriate department for approval

**Success Criteria:**

- Invoice is processed within 24 hours
- All required fields are populated correctly
- No errors occur during processing

## Scenario: Collection Call to Overdue Account

**Description:** Make collection calls to customers with overdue payments.

**Preconditions:**

- Customer account has overdue payment
- Collection call schedule is set

**Postconditions:**

- Customer is contacted via phone
- Payment plan or resolution is discussed
- Call details are logged in CRM

**Success Criteria:**

- Call is made within 48 hours of overdue date
- Customer responds positively
- Follow-up actions are documented

## Scenario: Dispute Resolution for Billing Issue

**Description:** Handle disputes raised by customers regarding billed amounts.

**Preconditions:**

- Customer has raised a dispute
- Dispute is logged in the system

**Postconditions:**

- Dispute is investigated
- Customer is notified of findings
- Resolution is documented

**Success Criteria:**

- Dispute is resolved within SLA period
- Customer is satisfied with resolution
- All documentation is complete
