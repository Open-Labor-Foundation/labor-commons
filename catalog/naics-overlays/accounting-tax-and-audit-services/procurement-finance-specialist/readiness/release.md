# Release Notes

### Features

- Complete workflow mapping for procurement finance specialists
- Comprehensive scenario coverage including small business, large enterprise, and emergency procurements
- Full alignment with FAR and GAO standards

- Fully compliant with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR)
