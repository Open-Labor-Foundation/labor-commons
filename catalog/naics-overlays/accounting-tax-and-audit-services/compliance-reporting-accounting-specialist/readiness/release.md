shows stable demand and the implementation has:

- retrieval bindings for ERP, GL, close, consolidation, reporting, disclosure-management, workpaper, PBC, tax-workflow, client portal, and approval systems;
- tenant-configured engagement scope, confidentiality, source-precedence, client-release, and reviewer-signoff rules;
- non-regression coverage for unsupported claims, conflicting source records, missing workpapers, confidentiality conflicts, and authority escalation;
- governance approval confirming the runtime still refuses legal, attestation, tax-position, editorial, contractual, and final disclosure-certification authority.
